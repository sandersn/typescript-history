FAQ

Q: How can I download the latest official TypeScript release?

You can download TypeScript as an npm package or a Windows Installer file (msi) from the Downloads page.

Q: What is the official TypeScript website?

You can find us on the internet at http://www.typescriptlang.org.

Q: How do I contribute to TypeScript?

For information on contributing, see How to Contribute to TypeScript.

Q: Can I incorporate TypeScript into my application?

TypeScript is released under the open source Apache 2.0 license. Any use allowed under that license is perfectly okay.
